===== Many-body physics, Quantum computing and quantum machine learning =====

o How to use many-body theory to design quantum circuits and gates (Quantum engineering), see for example URL:"https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.11694"
o Many-body theory with focus on
  * Adaptive basis sets 
  * Time dependence
  * Feedback from experiment
o Studies of many-body entanglement and quantum tomography
o Numerical experiments to mimick real systems, quantum twins
o Constructing quantum circuits to simulate specific systems
o Quantum machine learning to optimize quantum circuits
