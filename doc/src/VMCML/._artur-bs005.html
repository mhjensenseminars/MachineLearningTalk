Definitions

The one-body density matrix of the correlated system can be expressed in terms of these occupation numbers \( n_{\alpha} \) and the corresponding natural orbits \( \phi^{no}_{\alpha} \) by $$ \rho(r,r') = \sum_{\alpha} n_{\alpha} {\phi^{no}_{\alpha}}^*(r) \phi^{no}_{\alpha}(r') %\tag{1} $$
