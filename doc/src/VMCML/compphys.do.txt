institutt). Master og PhD variant.  KJM5560 har ikke vært en god nok
erstatning for fysikkstudenter og undervises også sporadisk.

!eblock

===== Important Issues to think of =====
o Lots of conceptual learning: superposition, entanglement, QIS applications, AI, machine learning
o Coding is indispensable. That is why this should be a part of a CS/DS program/Computational Physics
o Teamwork, project management, and communication are important and highly valued
o Engagement with industry: guest lectures, virtual tours, co-ops, and/or internships.
o Mentorship should begin the moment students enroll.

!split
===== Observations =====

o Students do not really know what QIS is. This may be important when advertising
o There is conflation of “Quantum Information Science” with “Quantum computing”.
o Students perceive that a graduate degree is necessary to work in QIS. A BSc will help.
o Students perceive AI/ML as central topics in modern science

!split
===== Future Needs/Problems =====

o There are already (USA) great needs for specialized people (Ph. D. s, postdocs), but also needs of  people with a broad overview of what is possible in QIS and AI/ML.
o There are not enough potential employees in QIS and AI/ML. It is a supply gap, not a skills gap.
o A BSc with specialization  is a good place to start
o It is tremendously important to get everyone speaking the same language. Facility with the vernacular of quantum mechanics and AI/ML is a big plus.
