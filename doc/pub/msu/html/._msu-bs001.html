Nuclear theory at FRIB

  1. Approximately 60-80 researchers and staff, faculty (12), graduate students, undergraduate students and post-doctoral fellows
  2. Research spans from fundamental symmetries and quantum chromodynamics, via effective field theories to nuclei and infinite matter (cold or hot and dense nuclear matter)
  3. Central in establishing and running the FRIB-theory alliance
